When a user enters our app, seamless authentication becomes the key to their personalized experience. Using the power of [Google OAuth2](https://developers.google.com/identity/protocols/oauth2), we establish user identity efficiently.

Upon successful login, Google OAuth2 delivers a `token_id` â€“ a unique identifier. Think of it as the user's digital fingerprint. Okto relies on this `token_id` for wallet authentication and, if needed, for creating new wallet.

To streamline the Google OAuth2 process, we're integrating the [@react-native-google-signin](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@react-native-google-signin/google-signin) library. This package simplifies the handling of authentication, making it a smooth experience for both users and developers alike.
